Team Leader / Advisor

Leading or advising a multi-disciplinary dam safety team — providing technical direction, quality review, and regulatory interface on longer engagements.

Training Programme

1–3 day state-level training for dam safety officials — DSA 2021, risk assessment, inspection methodology, EAP preparation, DHARMA portal.

Periodic technical advisory for dam owners managing multiple specified dams — ongoing support for compliance, inspections, and regulatory queries.

Post-failure or post-incident forensic investigation — field assessment, failure mechanism analysis, findings documentation.

◇
DPR Review

Technical examination and review of Detailed Project Reports for dams, barrages, and canal systems — hydraulic, structural, and regulatory aspects.
