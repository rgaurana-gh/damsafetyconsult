The Consultant

Why I Do
This Work

Dam safety is not an abstract regulatory exercise. India has over 6,000 large dams, many built decades ago, now facing increased hydrological loads, ageing infrastructure, and growing downstream populations. The Dam Safety Act 2021 has created the regulatory architecture — but implementation requires practitioners who understand both the law and the engineering.

After 35 years designing these structures and then helping regulate them, I bring both sides of that equation. The goal is not compliance paperwork — it is dams that do not fail.
