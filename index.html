What We Do

End-to-End Dam Safety
Services

From regulatory compliance under DSA 2021 to forensic investigation and training — rigorous, field-tested expertise across the full dam safety lifecycle.

01
DSA 2021 Compliance & CDSE Reports

Comprehensive dam safety assessments, CDSE report preparation, and regulatory compliance support under India's Dam Safety Act 2021 framework — for dam owners, SDSOs, and NDSA-designated structures.

02
Dam Break Analysis & EAP Preparation

Breach parameter estimation, inundation mapping, and Emergency Action Plan preparation. Aligned with NDSA Hazard & Vulnerability Classification Regulations 2024 (PCI-based H1–H6 system).

03
IDF Review & PMF Studies

Design flood adequacy assessment for dams and barrages — SPF vs. PMF determination, flood frequency analysis, and CWC Manual compliance. Specialist experience with Himalayan river hydrology.

04
Barrage & Hydraulic Structure Design

Hydraulic and structural design of barrages, stilling basins, canal systems, cross-drainage works per IS codes (IS 6512, IS 12720, IS 1904). Self-developed MS Excel design sheets for rapid analysis.

05
Dam Inspection & Forensic Investigation

Rapid risk assessment using World Bank/DRIP methodology. Dam safety inspections (visual, geotechnical, instrumentation). Forensic investigation of dam failures — including lead role in Luti Dam failure inquiry (Chhattisgarh).

06
Training & Capacity Building

State-level training for dam safety officials on DSA 2021, rapid risk assessment, DHARMA portal, and EAP methodology. Delivery experience across HP, Uttarakhand, Manipur and nationwide for NDSA/World Bank.

Key Projects &
Assignments

A selection of significant technical assignments spanning design, safety assessment, and forensic investigation across India's major river basins.

Dam Safety · Forensic
Luti Dam Failure Investigation, Chhattisgarh

Led rapid forensic investigation of dam failure on behalf of NDSA. Post-failure cause analysis and findings documentation.

Barrage Design · Jharkhand
Kharkai Barrage & Canal System

234-m barrage on river Kharkai for municipal and agricultural water supply. Full hydraulic and structural design including Larinier-type vertical-slot fish pass.

HEP · Uttarakhand

Main barrage and all appurtenant structures for underground powerhouse on river Dhauliganga, Chamoli district. NTPC Limited.

HEP · Uttarakhand
Koteshwar HEP Powerhouse Complex

Design consultancy for intake, powerhouse, and tailrace structures. Resolved special technical challenges during design and construction phases.

Inter-State Link · Gujarat/Maharashtra
Par-Tapi-Narmada Link Project

7 dams, ~400 km conveyance system including tunnels. Detailed design of link canal and appurtenant structures; DPR preparation for NWDA.

Irrigation · Uttar Pradesh
Badaun Lift Irrigation Project

102 cumecs diversion from Narora Barrage (Ganga), 15-m lift, 52 km canal system irrigating ~1.37 lakh ha across Badaun and Sambhal districts.

Diversion of surplus monsoon flows from Lahchura Dam (river Dhasan) to augment three dams. Kabrai Dam raised by 9.23 m to irrigate 44,381 ha additionally.
