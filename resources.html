Dam Safety
Reference Library

A curated collection of freely available dam safety references — Indian regulations, government manuals, international guidelines, and institutional portals. Organised for practitioners, not academics.

Copyright note: Only freely distributable documents are linked or hosted here — Government of India publications, US Government documents (public domain), World Bank open-access reports, and similar. Commercial textbooks, BIS/IS codes, and journal papers are listed with links to official sources for purchase or institutional access. Do not upload or share copyrighted material.
Indian Regulatory Framework

DSA 2021 & NDSA

The primary statutory and regulatory documents governing dam safety in India — all freely available from government sources.

⊙
Dam Safety Act, 2021 — Gazette Notification
The primary legislation. Defines specified dams, establishes NDSA and NCDS, mandates obligations of dam owners and SDSOs.
FreeGoI GazetteSection 2(x) · Chapter III · V

Status of dam safety implementation across India. Authored by Er. Rakesh Gaurana during NDSA consultancy tenure.

⊙
NDSA Vulnerability & Hazard Classification Regulations, 2024
PCI-based H1–H6 hazard classification system for specified dams. Governs EAP requirements and downstream consequence assessment.

US Government Publications

USBR · USACE · FEMA

US Government dam safety publications are public domain and freely distributable. These are the most widely referenced international technical manuals in dam safety practice globally.

◈
USBR — Design of Small Dams (3rd Edition)
The definitive reference for small dam design — embankment dams, spillways, outlets, stilling basins, and safety evaluation. Widely used in India alongside IS codes.
Public DomainUSBRDesign
Download PDF →
◈
USBR — Embankment Dam Instrumentation Manual
Comprehensive guide to instrumentation of embankment dams — piezometers, settlement gauges, inclinometers, seepage measurement, and data interpretation.
Public DomainUSBRInstrumentation
USBR Website →
◈
USACE EM 1110-2-1603 — Hydraulic Design of Spillways
USACE Engineer Manual on spillway hydraulic design — ogee profile, discharge coefficients, energy dissipation. Widely used alongside IS 6512 and CWC guidelines.
Public DomainUSACEHydraulics
USACE Pubs →
◈
USACE EM 1110-2-2702 — Design of Spillway Tainter Gates
Engineering manual for tainter (radial) gate design — trunnion loads, pier design, HAB, and structural design. Reference used in Indian barrage gate design alongside IS 11572.
Public DomainUSACEGates
USACE Pubs →
◈
FEMA — Federal Guidelines for Dam Safety
FEMA's comprehensive guidelines covering dam safety programmes, emergency action planning, hazard classification, and safety inspections. Frequently referenced for international practice comparison.
Public DomainFEMAEAP
FEMA Dam Safety →
◈
FEMA — Selecting and Accommodating Inflow Design Floods for Dams
Guidance on IDF selection methodology — risk-based approaches, PMF determination, and the rationale for IDF standards. Useful reference alongside CWC and IS 11223 for IDF review work.
Public DomainFEMAIDF · PMF
FEMA →
International — Australia · UK · Canada

International Guidelines

Leading international dam safety frameworks — freely available from ANCOLD, Environment Agency UK, and CDA Canada.

⊕
ANCOLD — Guidelines on Dam Safety Management (Australia)
Australian National Committee on Large Dams guidelines — risk-based dam safety management framework. One of the most advanced national frameworks, widely referenced internationally for ALARP and risk tolerability.
ANCOLD Member AccessAustraliaRisk-Based
ANCOLD →
⊕
Environment Agency UK — Reservoir Safety Guidance
UK reservoir safety framework under the Reservoirs Act — inspection requirements, qualified civil engineer panels, and safety reporting. Good reference for comparison with DSA 2021 inspection regime.
FreeUKInspection
Gov.uk →
⊕
CDA — Dam Safety Guidelines (Canadian Dam Association)
Canadian Dam Association technical bulletin on dam safety — risk assessment, consequence classification, and safety review. Strong on risk-informed decision making for dam safety.
CDA Member AccessCanadaRisk Assessment
CDA →
⊕
ICOLD — International Commission on Large Dams
ICOLD Bulletins on dam safety, risk assessment, seismic analysis, instrumentation, and EAP. Some bulletins freely available; others require member access through national committee (INCOLD for India).
Some Free · Some MemberICOLDInternational
ICOLD →
